# PROJECT RULES

## Rule 1

There is only ONE Primary Project at a time.

---

## Rule 2

Every new idea goes to IDEA_BANK.md.

It does NOT interrupt the current project.

---

## Rule 3

Every project must answer:

- Why am I doing this?
- Who benefits?
- Does it move me toward my life vision?

---

## Rule 4

Never depend on memory.

Document everything important.

---

## Rule 5

Small progress every day is better than waiting for motivation.

---

## Rule 6

Before starting anything new:

Finish...
Pause...
or Archive...
the current project.
