# DECISION ENGINE

## Purpose

Help Mostafa make better decisions based on his life vision instead of temporary emotions.

---

# Decision Framework

Every important decision must answer these questions:

## 1. Does it move me toward financial freedom?

Score: /10

---

## 2. Does it help my family?

Score: /10

---

## 3. Does it teach me a valuable skill?

Score: /10

---

## 4. Does it create a long-term asset?

Score: /10

---

## 5. Does it fit my life vision?

Score: /10

---

## 6. Will I still be happy I made this decision after 5 years?

Score: /10

---

# Decision Result

50–60 → Excellent Decision

40–49 → Good Decision

30–39 → Think Again

Below 30 → Avoid
